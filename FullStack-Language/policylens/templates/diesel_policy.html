{% trans "What is the Diesel Subsidy Policy?" %}

{% blocktrans %} In June 2024, Malaysia implemented a targeted diesel subsidy policy aimed at reducing government expenditure and ensuring that subsidies benefit those most in need. Prior to this reform, diesel was heavily subsidized, leading to significant financial burdens on the government. The cost of diesel subsidies escalated from RM1.4 billion in 2019 to RM14.3 billion in 2023. {% endblocktrans %}
