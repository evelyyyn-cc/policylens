Diesel Subsidy Malaysia

Date : June 2024

In June 2024, Malaysia implemented a targeted diesel subsidy policy aimed at reducing government expenditure and ensuring that subsidies benefit those most in need. Prior to this reform, diesel was heavily subsidized, leading to significant financial burdens on the government.
