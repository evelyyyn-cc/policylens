Why it Matters?

Government policies shape the prices we pay, the jobs we pursue, and the lives we lead.

But when policies are buried in jargon or distorted by headlines, citizens lose trust and power.

PolicyLens helps you cut through the noise, understand what's really happening, and make decisions that matter.

Who We Are?

We're a group of designers, developers, and researchers from Monash University's School of IT, brought together by a shared frustration: public policies are hard to understand, but they affect all of us.

So we built PolicyLens, a platform that simplifies complex policies into clear, actionable insights for everyday people.

We believe informed citizens are empowered citizens and that starts with clarity.
